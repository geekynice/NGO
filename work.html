Activities

WANGO regularly participates in major world conferences sponsored by the UN and other international and intergovernmental organizations, as well as works in partnership with UN member states and NGOs to organize events on issues of mutual concern.

Work with USAID - WANGO session

May 11, 2021 Webinar

This webinar hosted by the U.S. Agency for International Development (USAID), in collaboration with WANGO, presented a brief history and overview of USAID, the guiding principles and methods of the New Partnerships Initiative, and ways organizations can find funding opportunities.

Intellectual Leadership Platform: Series 1: Third Sector

March 27, 2021 Webinar

The Trans Atlantic Center, in collaboration with WANGO, held this webinar aimed at developing the skills and talents of leaders in the nonprofit, public, and private sectors in Nigeria as a means to support sustainable development in the country.

WAAS@60

February 15-19, 2021 Online conference

This virtual conference organized by the World Academy of Art & Science (WAAS), in partnership with WANGO and 30 other organizations, featured sessions covering a wide range of issues concerning the complementary contributions of art and science to knowledge, human rights, human security, and well-being.

15th Global Forum on Human Settlements

October 15-16, 2020 Online conference

The Global Forum on Human Settlements (GFHS), in cooperation with WANGO, the World Meterological Organization (WMO), the International Telecommunication Union (ITU), the International Union for Conservation of Nature (IUCN), and more than 15 other international organizations, virtually convened its 15th annual meeting in observance of Urban October.

Global Leadership in the 21st Century

June 15-19, 2020 Online Conference

The United Nations Office at Geneva (UNOG) and The World Academy of Art & Science (WAAS), in partnership with WANGO and more than 20 other international organizations, held this virtual conference to foster the emergence of the global leadership needed to address the complex, pressing challenges facing humanity today.
